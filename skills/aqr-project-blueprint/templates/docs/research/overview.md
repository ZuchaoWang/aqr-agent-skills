# Research overview

Background research, related-work comparisons, and exploratory notes. Research docs inform design decisions; they are not specs. A research note may be marked `archived` once its conclusions have been folded into `architecture/` or `implementation/`.

## 2. When to add a research note

Add a research note when:

- A design decision needs context the architecture doc does not carry.
- The team is comparing approaches and the comparison is non-trivial.
- External work (papers, prior art, vendor docs) is being cited.

Do not add a research note for routine implementation choices — those belong in the implementation doc.
